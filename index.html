About Us

Consultant For Animal Husbandary Farming And Veterinary Services

Foresight Agrovet is a consultancy firm which carries out various activities related to animal husbandry development, mainly solving various problems faced by the farmers related to goat and sheep rearing. This includes mainly goat and sheep rearing training, online consultation to the farmers as well as a complete YouTube channel called "Complete Goat Farming" to make the goat rearing business easier and through this technical information available to the cattle breeders. In this, about 130 videos on goat farming have been made available to the farmers. Various such activities are carried out for the benefit of the pastorals through this organization.
